HOW TO TRACK YOUR ORDERS FROM RRQ STORE

Step-by-Step Order Tracking

  1. Login to Your Account
    Go to RRQ Store and click on the Account icon at the top right. Sign in with your registered email.
  2. Open "My Orders"
    After logging in, navigate to My Profile or Orders section from your account menu.
  3. Select Your Order
    You'll see a list of all your recent orders. Click on the order you want to track.
  4. Check Status & Tracking Number
    Inside the order detail, you will find the status (Processing, Shipped, Completed, etc.) and the tracking number (resi).
  5. Track with Courier
    Copy your tracking number and visit the official courier website (JNE, SiCepat, J&T, etc.) to get real-time updates on your package.
**Note:** You will also receive a shipment email with your tracking info when your order is shipped.
